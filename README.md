Stay updated on warhammer-community.com news directly on your Discord server! Powered by discord.js, Da Postork allows you and your server to receive the latest updates from warhammer-community.com without leaving your server.

## Getting Started
To add Da Postork to your discord server, simply click [here](https://discord.com/api/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1161725703931297886&permissions=84032&scope=bot).
